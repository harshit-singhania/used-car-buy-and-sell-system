Frequently Asked Questions

How do I list my car for sale?

Login as a seller, open Add New Car, fill in all required details, and submit. Your listing appears immediately on buyer pages.

How is the Car Health Score calculated?

The score uses age and mileage: 100 - (currentYear - carYear) * 3 - (mileage / 10000) * 2, then clamps between 0 and 100.

What does the Price Alert warning mean?

Listings are flagged when the price deviates more than 40% from the average for cars with the same make and year.

How do I contact a seller?

Open car details and use the Contact Seller button to view seller phone and email information.

Can I cancel a purchase?

This demo project does not support purchase cancellation yet. Please contact support to raise a request.
